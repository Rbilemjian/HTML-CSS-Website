Food is more than just a means of survival. It is a cultural expression. An artistic medium. A true delicacy. Here are some great restaurants that I believe will blow your socks off, and put you in a state of satisfaction after which you just may never truly be the same.

Doomies

A plant-based joint filled with delicious but unhealthy food.

Menu Highlights

Big Mac
Probably the GOAT. Two patties, can be any combination of beef/chicken. Special sauce. The works.
Philly Cheesesteak
Very salty stuff, but the best cheese/beef combination you will find in plant-based foods.
Crispy Chicken
Classic. Sort of boring, but gets the job done and is a guaranteed quality sandwich.
Peanutbutter & Bacon
Just try it. Don't think about it. Trust me.

Pros

  • Delicious food
  • Open late
  • Pretty solid customer service

Cons

  • Sort of expensive
  • Pretty unhealthy stuff
  • In downtown, not the best area

Lum Ka Naad

A place with a variety of Asian cuisine on the menu, of which I have had two items despite being a regular there.

Menu Highlights

Drunken Noodle
Absolute MVP. Order with no spice, no egg, and a soy protein substitute and you have a plant-based macronutrient powerhouse.
Orange Chicken
Nice thing to get on the side. Replace the chicken with soy protein if you'd like.
White Rice
This may seem sort of plain to put into a menu highlights section, but it is literally the only other thing on the menu that I have tried. Gets a 8/10 from me.

Pros

  • Healthy food packed with macronutrients needed for a healthy diet.
  • Great bang for buck
  • Open late
  • Great customer service

Cons

  • Not the most delicious stuff out there
